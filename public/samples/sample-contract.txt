PROFESSIONAL SERVICES AGREEMENT

This Professional Services Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into on March 15, 2024, between HB Legal Intelligence Platform LLC ("Service Provider") and Acme Corporation ("Client").

1. SERVICES
Service Provider shall provide AI-powered document analysis and legal intelligence services, including but not limited to:
- Contract review and analysis
- Risk assessment and mitigation
- Compliance monitoring
- Document extraction and summarization

2. TERM
This Agreement shall commence on April 1, 2024, and continue until March 31, 2025, unless terminated earlier in accordance with the terms herein.

3. COMPENSATION
Client agrees to pay Service Provider $15,000 per month, payable within thirty (30) days of receipt of invoice.

4. TERMINATION
Either party may terminate this Agreement with ninety (90) days written notice.

5. LIABILITY
Service Provider's liability shall be limited to the amount paid by Client in the twelve (12) months preceding the claim, not to exceed $180,000.

6. CONFIDENTIALITY
Both parties acknowledge that they may receive confidential information and agree to maintain such information in confidence for a period of five (5) years following termination.

7. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
All work product created by Service Provider shall remain the property of Service Provider, with Client receiving a non-exclusive license to use.

8. GOVERNING LAW
This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of Missouri.
